# Karman Kar Shows & Events
# Staff Quick Reference Sheet (Event Day)

---

# 🚗 CAR OWNER CHECK-IN PROCESS

1. Hand pre-printed windshield sheet to car owner
2. Scan STAFF QR on the sheet
3. Enter:

   - Owner Name (required)
   - Phone (required)
   - Email (optional)
   - Year / Make / Model (required)
   - Marketing opt-in (optional)

4. Click Save
5. Confirm information appears correctly

---

# 📄 PAPER WAIVER PROCESS

1. Owner signs physical waiver
2. Staff logs into Admin panel
3. Mark waiver as received
4. Confirm timestamp saved

Waiver tracking ensures legal compliance.

---

# 🗳 VOTER PROCESS (Explain to Guests)

1. Scan QR code on windshield sheet
2. Choose vote quantity
3. Stripe checkout opens
4. Payment completes
5. Vote is recorded automatically

Important:
- $1 per vote
- Votes only count after payment success
- No payment = no vote recorded

---

# 🚨 COMMON ISSUES

## "Voting is closed"
→ Ask admin to reopen voting.

## Stripe page won't load
→ Confirm internet connection.

## Check-in data disappears
→ Refresh page.
→ Confirm Save was clicked.

---

# 🔐 DATA POLICY

- We do not sell data.
- Phone required only if owner opts into updates.
- Sponsors may only contact if attendee opted in.
